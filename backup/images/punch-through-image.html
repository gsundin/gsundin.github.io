    Expertise, platforms, and tools trusted by over 400,000 developers.

    The industry-leading BLE test app for iOS. Used by over 350,000 people, LightBlue Explorer lets you scan, connect to and browse any nearby Bluetooth Smart device. Includes full support for logging data and simulating peripherals. Available for free on the App Store.

    The LightBlue Bean is a Bluetooth enabled development board for the mobile age. Program Bean and Bean+ wirelessly over BLE from Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android. Use LightBlue Explorer to interact with the Bean over BLE, or use Bean's iOS and Android SDKs to quickly build a custom app. Great for prototyping connected products.
